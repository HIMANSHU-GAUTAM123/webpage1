About Me


Hi, I'm Himanshu Gautam a Computer Science student at LNMIIT jaipur , India . I am a self taught programmer having interset in Front-End Development & competitive coding . I enjoy working closely with team members to ensure workloads are effectively redirected to bottlenecks and personally picking up the remaining slack . I have passion for both personal growth and software development , I am ready to apply my passion for coding to a talented engineering team to develop quality solutions.

Hobbies


My hobby is collecting quotations of great personalities like Gandhiji, Abhram Lincoln, Kalidas, Napoleon, Chankya etc.I collect quotations while reading books, newspapers and magazines in my leisure. I feel relief and more zeal after reading the quotations. Quotations give a disappointed person a lot of inspiration and courage and help him to prepare himself for doing something new.In my opinion, when a boy or girl passes the age of 18 to 25 he or she has to build up his or her career as many difficulties crop up in his/her way. He/she loses hope and gets discouraged these qualities playing a great role in inspiring him/ her to try and try again until he/she achieves his/her goal. The quotations play a great role in inspiring him/her to try and try and try again until he/she achieves his/her goal. The quotations are the experiences of great personalities. So according to me a person, who follows them, cannot fail in any field of life.
